Why submit an application?

Think of it as a "Shark Tank" opportunity to get your idea in front of new potential customers and collaborators, and to receive feedback.

How does it work?

Complete the application form (link below) by October 15, 2019. A Review Committee will select the 10 best applicants and notify participants by October 22, 2019. You must also agree to submitting your slides one week in advance of the webinar on Dec. 4 or Dec. 11, 2019. You will present remotely, using our webinar platform. Each company will be limited to one presenter.

How much does it cost you?

There is no cost to submit an application! If you are among the 10 selected presenters, there is a cost of $2,000 to defray expenses, payable within 10 days of notification (due by November 1, 2019).

Why are we doing this?

At HealthEconomics.Com, we are committed to innovation and change in HEOR, RWE and Health Technology and are constantly striving to strengthen our CONNECTED COMMUNITY™. We desire to give a platform to you, if your ideas are innovative and change-worthy, so that we can all do our part to improve healthcare across the globe. And, of course, we hope you continue to remain clients of HealthEconomics.Com and use our webinars and advertising resources to connect with our 25,000-strong community.

How do I increase my chances of being selected?

There will be a rigorous evaluation of the finalists chosen, based on several criteria:

  • New: The company, product, or service must be new (3 years or younger).
  • Innovation: The idea must be different, innovating and disrupting.
  • Relevant: Demonstrate, with actual data or case studies, how the idea can be used to facilitate meaningful change to health care, medicine, patients, payers, providers, or our industry.
  • Clarity: Tell your story with simplicity and impact.
  • Completeness: Fill out the form completely. Incomplete applications will not be evaluated.
