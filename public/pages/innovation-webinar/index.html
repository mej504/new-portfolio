HE Innovation Series

Big Ideas Accelerating Change in HEOR,
RWE and Health Technology

HealthEconomics.Com launched its first-ever HE Innovation Webinar Series on Big Ideas Accelerating Change in HEOR, RWE and Health Technology. The online event brought disruptors — people, ideas and companies — who believed in innovation and change for the betterment of patients and health care.

In a 2-webinar series, nine of the most disrupting companies presented a 10-minute pitch via webinar to hundreds of HEOR, MA, and RWE researchers, payers, and industry movers-and-shakers. The audience voted on their favorite innovator. View both webinars on-demand: Webinar 1 | Webinar 2
